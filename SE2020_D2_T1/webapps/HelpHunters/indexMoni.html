Register as a Caregiver (REGISTER-CARETAKERS.html)
Description:
This function shows a form to enter information as caregiver who wants to work within the app. When the caregiver submits the information, it will be stored in the database but can't be shown until the administrators accept this worker.
Use:
In the bottom of INICIO.html you'll find a link to this form. You have to fill the fields and submit the data.
caregiverData.java Inserts data on table Caregiver
caregiverRegister.java Takes the information from the form and saves it in the data constructor so it can be inserted in the table Caregiver
